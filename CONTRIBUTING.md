# Contributing to CIPHER.SYS

We welcome contributions from the community to help make **CIPHER.SYS** the ultimate terminal-based productivity machine.

## How to Contribute

1.  **Fork** the repository on GitHub.
2.  **Clone** your fork to your local machine.
3.  **Create a branch** for your feature or bug fix.
4.  **Commit** your changes with clear, descriptive messages.
5.  **Push** your branch to your fork.
6.  **Open a Pull Request** against the `main` branch.

## Standards
- **Aesthetics**: Maintain the high-contrast, CRT-inspired visual style.
- **Privacy**: No telemetry, no cloud logging. Keep it phantom.
- **Performance**: Ensure the mDNS discovery and socket communication remain lightweight.
